What Drives My Passion

I’m a backend-focused Java developer with hands-on experience in building scalable systems using tools like Spring Boot, Hibernate, and Docker. I care about clean code and maintainability — sometimes even obsessing over proper indentation.

I'm actively exploring cloud-native development and microservices, with a growing interest in tools like RabbitMQ and containerized workflows. I implement CI/CD pipelines in both professional and open-source contexts to streamline deployments and improve reliability.

Outside of coding, I enjoy aerial photography and experimenting with Raspberry Pi and electronics. I occasionally dive into 3D printing, usually building odd but fun gadgets.
